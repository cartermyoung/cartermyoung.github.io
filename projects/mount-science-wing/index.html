Mount Saint Joseph Academy Construction and Renovation Projects

Project summary

Managed various new construction and renovation projects around the Mount’s sprawling Flourtown campus. Installed new HVAC systems throughout the academic wing and completely renovated laboratories and classrooms in the science department. Built a new multi-purpose turf athletic field and school store. Refurnished the school auditorium, replaced front entrance sculpture, made renovations to the Fontbonne Room, reroofed several buildings, and resurfaced the tennis courts.

  • Acted as the owner’s representative during the design and construction of all projects over a six year period
  • Completed the due diligence and prequalification work for all the architects, consulting engineering firms, construction managers, and general contractors required on the projects
  • Wrote RFP’s and handled all proposal evaluations, descope meetings, contract award meetings, and contract negotiations
  • Attended all preconstruction and construction phase meetings representing the school in all aspects
  • Managed the project scheduling, all required change order review and negotiations, budgeting and contractor payment applications, and all township and state agency submittals and approvals
  • Completed all project close out procedures and procurement of warranties and guarantees
  • All projects completed on time and within budget (many done over tight time frames during the school’s summer break)

Budget

$4.75M

Timeframe

January 2013 - September 2019

Location

Flourtown, PA
