Project summary

Managed various new construction and renovation projects around the Mount’s sprawling Flourtown campus. Installed new HVAC systems throughout the academic wing and completely renovated laboratories and classrooms in the science department. Built a new multi-purpose turf athletic field and school store. Refurnished the school auditorium, replaced front entrance sculpture, made renovations to the Fontbonne Room, reroofed several buildings, resurfaced the tennis courts and track. Renovated the school cafeteria and food preparation areas as well as restrooms throughout the facility.

  • Acted as the owner’s representative during the design and construction of all projects over a nine year period
  • Completed the due diligence and prequalification work for all the architects, consulting engineering firms, construction managers, and general contractors required on the projects
  • Wrote RFPs and handled all proposal evaluations, descope meetings, contract award meetings, and contract negotiations
  • Attended all preconstruction and construction phase meetings representing the school in all aspects
  • Managed the project scheduling, all required change order review and negotiations, budgeting and contractor payment applications, and all township and state agency submittals and approvals
  • Completed all project close out procedures and procurement of warranties and guarantees
  • All projects completed on time and within budget (many done over tight time frames during the school’s summer break)
  • Authored and distributed the RFPs for the procurement of a new facility management team and a new custodial firm
  • Authored and distributed to pre-selected architectural firms the RFP for the school’s future “Master Plan”

Budget

$7.0M

Timeframe

Location
