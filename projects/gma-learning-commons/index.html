Gwynedd Mercy Academy High School Renovations

Project Summary

New academic wing HVAC systems and library renovation

  • Managed, coordinated, and supervised the installation of new air conditioning systems throughout the school’s academic wing.
    • Received RFPs from pre-qualified engineering firms, awarded design work, and completed contracts.
    • Solicited firm price proposals from an invited list of pre-approved mechanical contractors.
  • Renovated the existing library into a state-of-the-art learning commons.
    • Worked with architectural design firm to develop construction plans and specifications.
    • Worked with the school’s facilities committee and architect to collect firm price bids.
  • Managed communications between architects, contractors, school administration, and facilities department.
  • Conducted pre-planning and formulated pre-construction schedule and techniques.
  • Reviewed and approved all change orders.
  • Both projects were completed on-time within a tight timeframe during the school’s twelve week summer recess.

Budget

$1.18M

Timeframe

May 2014 - August 2015

Location

Gwynedd Valley, PA
