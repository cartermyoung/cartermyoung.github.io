Carter M. Young of Blue Bell, Pennsylvania passed in the presence of his loving wife Tuesday February 7, 2023.

Beloved husband of 51 years to Margaret; loving father to Ryan Young (Katie) and Kirsten Henry (Joe), and dear grandfather to Finnegan Henry.

Visitation 10 AM Friday at Upper Dublin Lutheran Church - udlc.org; Service to follow at 11 AM.

Interment private.

In lieu of flowers, donations are appreciated. Committee to Benefit the Children - cbckids.org
