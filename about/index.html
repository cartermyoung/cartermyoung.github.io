About

Professional Summary

An owner’s representative with experience in commercial, industrial, and institutional construction project management. Has worked in both union and open shop markets. Completed a portfolio of projects worth over $700M for some of the Philadelphia area’s leading corporations, financial institutions, utilities, schools, hospitals, non-profit, and charitable organizations. A former contracting firm Vice President with a civil engineering degree. Accessible, experienced, fair, and willing to go beyond expectations to deliver your project on-time and within budget.
